How it works

01
Message the agent — describe the job in plain language, like you're texting a colleague on site.
02
AI breaks it down — extracts scope, quantities, materials and labour. Cross-references current supplier rates and historical data.
03
Receive an itemised quote — line-by-line with totals, GST, timeline and target margin. Under 15 seconds.
04
Accept and send — one tap to send to client and log in the roen pipeline automatically.
