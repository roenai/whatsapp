How it works

01
Describe the change — text or voice from site. What changed, why, and what's affected.
02
AI calculates impact — cost delta, schedule impact, affected trades. Cross-references current contract and rates.
03
Review the CO — formal change order with all fields. Cost breakdown, justification, approval chain.
04
Route for approval — one tap to send to client/owner for signature. Logged against contract automatically.
